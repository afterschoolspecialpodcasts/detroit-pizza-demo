Our Story

A pan, a dream, and a city that believed in something different.

2011 Est. Detroit
How It Started

A Pan Found in a Flea Market Changed Everything.

"I found a battered automotive steel pan in a Eastern Market flea market. Something about its weight told me it was meant to hold more than machine parts."

In 2011, Marcus Washington brought home that pan, seasoned it with oil, and baked his first Detroit-style pie for his family in the Midtown neighborhood he'd grown up in. Within a week, the whole block was knocking on his door.

What started in a home kitchen became a food truck, then a restaurant, then a Detroit institution. Today, Steel Pan Pizza Co. operates three locations across metro Detroit — each one honoring that original pan and the city that raised it.

What We Stand For

Our Craft, Our Code.

🔥
Craft Over Convenience

We never pre-make, never freeze, never cut corners. Every pan is built from scratch, every day. It takes longer. It's worth it.

🏭
Detroit Through & Through

We source from Michigan suppliers whenever possible. From our flour to our dairy to our beer — we believe in building up the city that built us.

Community First

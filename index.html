Our Story

Why Steel Pan?

Detroit-style pizza isn't a trend we chased — it's what we grew up eating. The steel pan is the whole point. Those heavy, well-seasoned rectangular pans are what give every slice its signature: a thick, airy dough that's crisp and almost fried on the sides where Wisconsin brick cheese melts all the way to the edge, caramelizing into a lacey, crunchy crust that no other pizza style can replicate. That's the detail we obsess over.

We started out of a small kitchen on the east side, making pies for neighbors and family who kept asking for more. The recipes haven't changed. We still proof the dough twice, still use the same brick cheese blend, still ladle the sauce on top the way Detroit always does it — sauce over cheese, never under. Every pan is pressed by hand before it goes in the oven.

Detroit had a rough few decades, but the food never lost its soul. Steel Pan Pizza Co. is our small way of putting something real back into the Motor City — a pizza that tastes like it belongs here, made by people who do.

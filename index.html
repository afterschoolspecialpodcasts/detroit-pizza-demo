Our Story

Pizza the way it
was meant to be.

Steel Pan Pizza Co. was born in Royal Oak from a love of Detroit's iconic square pizza — thick, airy dough baked in seasoned steel pans until the edges caramelize into a shattering, golden crust.

Every pie starts with our Signature Cheese Blend, finished with imported aged Italian cheese blend, and crafted fresh to order. We sell out every single night.

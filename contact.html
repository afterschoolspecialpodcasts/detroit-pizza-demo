FAQ

Common Questions

🕐

How long does a deep dish take?

Deep dish pies are baked to order and typically take 25–35 minutes from the time you order. It's worth every second.

🍽

Do you offer gluten-free crust?

Yes, at all three locations for an additional $4. Prepared in a designated area to reduce cross-contact.

🚚

What's your delivery radius?

We deliver within 5 miles of each location. Orders outside this range can arrange carry-out or catering delivery.

🋿

Do you take reservations?

We're walk-in only for dine-in — no reservations needed. Large parties of 10+ should call ahead to ensure seating availability.

Still Hungry for Answers?

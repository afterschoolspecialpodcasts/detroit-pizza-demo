For smaller events (under 30 people), 72 hours is usually enough. For larger events, please give us at least one week — and for weddings or corporate events over 100 guests, two weeks is ideal.
Midtown has street parking plus a validated rear lot. Corktown has free street parking and an adjacent city lot. Ferndale has a dedicated 40-space free lot attached to the building.
We take walk-ins at all locations. Reservations are available for parties of 6 or more — call your preferred location directly to book. We always hold space for walk-ins, even on busy nights.
